Ford Active Grille Shutters are special shutters in the grill that are opened and closed automatically to reduce aerodynamic drag while still allowing for proper cooling. When the shutters are closed aerodynamics are improved by up to 6%, resulting in improved fuel economy. When the engine needs additional cooling the shutters open to allow more air to flow through the radiator, and the shutters then close again automatically when conditions allow.
