This vehicle comes standard with front wheel drive. It features a 3.5 Liter T.i. V.C.T. 24 valve dual overhead cams V. 6 engine. with a 6 speed electronically controlled Select Shift automatic overdrive trans axle type. It has 288 horsepower at 6500 revolutions per minute, with 254 torque foot pounds at 4000 revolutions per minute, while both using 93 octane. The E.P.A estimates 19 miles per gallon in the city, 29 miles per gallon on the highway, or 23 miles per gallon combined. It has a minimum recommended 87 octane fuel type. It features a naturally aspirated induction system, with sequential multi port electronic fuel injection fuel delivery, a 10.8 to 1 compression ratio, and direct acting mechanical bucket valve lifters. It has a beautiful duel stainless steel with catalytic converter exhaust. The engine block and cylinder head material are both cast aluminum.
