WALK-AROUND


Highlights

This vehicle has a 5 person max seating capacity. It comes with front wheel drive. It features a 3.5 Liter T.i. V.C.T. 24 valve dual overhead cams V. 6 engine. With a 6 speed electronically controlled Select Shift automatic overdrive transmission. It has a large 20.1 cubic feet trunk compartment with rear fold down seats. It has a beautiful duel stainless steel with catalytic converter exhaust. It has four 19” premium painted aluminum wheels, and Electric Power-Assisted (EPAS) steering, with Four-wheel ABS (four channel); AdvanceTrac with ESC (Electronic Stability Control™) including Curve Control and Torque Vectoring Control. Its Front suspension has Independent, MacPherson struts with rear-facing lower L-arms with stabilizer bar. Its rear suspension has Multi-link fully independent suspension with coil-over shocks and stabilizer bar. Its rear shocks are Twin-tube, hydraulic, gas-pressurized. Features Active grill shutters. 13.86" front and rear brake rotors with 439 square centimeter swept area, and vented front discs, and solid rear discs, with 410 square centimeter swept area, all with comfort market brake linings. Power assist vacuum with brake assist, and torque vectoring control.
